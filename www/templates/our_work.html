Our Work

Life University is working towards promoting Holistic living based on the principle, “The whole is greater than the sum of its parts”.

Our Programs:

Many meditation and awareness programs have been started at Life University reaching out to the urban as well as rural population, adults and children.

Soul Coach Training Program

Dr. Newton Kondaveti, Founder, Life University brings to you a unique, Holistic Spiritual Training Program called ‘Soul Coach Training’. This training gives us a thorough understanding on all the aspects of Spirituality and also provides tools on how to integrate and apply it into Physical Life.

Shramdaan

Shramadaan at Life University aims at creating a Sangha of like-minded people conglomerating together to offer their hands on services on the land from time to time.

Maitreya Buddha Training Program:

This is an advanced level training program for Soul Coaches who are already certified in SCTP by Life University.

Little Buddhas Training Program:
The most awaited Little Buddhas Training is conducted by Dr. Newton Kondaveti on behalf of Life University every year for children between the age groups of 5 to 11 years to usher in soul science in the lives of our future generations.
